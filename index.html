01 · Challenge

Securing a fragmented coastal border.

A central-government client responsible for maritime security needed continuous, resilient surveillance across a fragmented coastal stretch — commercial-grade CCTV wouldn't survive the environment, and existing vendors couldn't meet the compliance, uptime, and documentation bar for a multi-crore government tender.

02 · Strategy

Custom wireless surveillance, engineered for compliance.

I built the opportunity from cold territory — mapped stakeholders, positioned a hardened wireless surveillance architecture tailored to the government's procurement standards, and drove the tender/RFP end-to-end with technical, compliance, and pricing teams. Won on capability plus a documentation trail that survived scrutiny.

03 · Impact

A multi-crore win, replicated across the coast.

Delivered a ₹6Cr+ contract on time and under strict regulatory compliance — then used the same playbook to open Chennai, Andhra Pradesh, Goa, and coastal Karnataka as new territories for the company. The engagement anchored the firm's presence in coastal & maritime security for years to follow.
